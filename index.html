Cybersecurity Career Transition

This website is designed to guide individuals with no technical background through a one-year journey to transition into cybersecurity careers.

The website will provide information on the following topics:

  • What is cybersecurity?
  • What are the different types of cybersecurity jobs?
  • What are the skills and qualifications needed for a cybersecurity career?
  • How to get started in a cybersecurity career?

The website will also provide a one-year journey plan that will guide users through the process of transitioning into a cybersecurity career.

The journey plan will include the following steps:

  1. Learn the basics of cybersecurity.
  2. Get certified in cybersecurity.
  3. Gain experience in cybersecurity.
  4. Network with other cybersecurity professionals.
  5. Apply for cybersecurity jobs.

The website will also provide resources to help users with each step of the journey plan.

These resources will include:

  • Online courses
  • Books
  • Webinars
  • Conferences
  • Mentorship programs

The website will be updated regularly with new content and resources.

I hope that this website will help you to transition into a cybersecurity career.
