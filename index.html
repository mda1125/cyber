All About Hummingbirds

Types of Hummingbirds

There are many species of hummingbirds, known for their vibrant colors and incredible agility. Some common types include the Ruby-throated Hummingbird, Anna's Hummingbird, and the Rufous Hummingbird. Each has unique characteristics and habitats.

Hummingbird Food Recipe

You can easily make nectar for hummingbirds at home!

  • Mix 1 part white granulated sugar with 4 parts boiling water.
  • Stir until the sugar is completely dissolved.
  • Let the mixture cool completely before filling your feeders.
  • Important: Do not use honey, brown sugar, or red dye, as these can be harmful to hummingbirds.
